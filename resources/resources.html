MathsJam Shout Posters Printable sign that says 'MathsJam' to put out at your event Printable MathsJam postcards - available in A4 and Letter sizes, to print on card and cut into four Useful links: MathsJam Photos Tumblr - please submit photos taken at your MathsJam here so we can collect a flavour of what's its like, and that you're happy for people to use to show what MathsJam is like MathsJam Slack - please join this Slack community to meet and chat with other MathsJam organisers Here's a blurb about MathsJam you can use to promote your event in emails: MathsJam is a monthly opportunity for like-minded self-confessed maths enthusiasts to get together in a pub and share stuff they like. Puzzles, games, problems, or just anything they think is cool or interesting. We meet on the second to last Tuesday of every month, in the evening, simultaneously in pubs and bars around the UK (and the world!).
